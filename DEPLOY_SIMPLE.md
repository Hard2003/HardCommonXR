## **THE EASIEST WAY - Railway.app (3 Clicks, 2 Minutes)**

### **That's it. No SSH. No Ubuntu. No terminal commands needed.**

### **Step 1: Go to Railway**
```
https://railway.app
```

### **Step 2: Click "Login with GitHub"**
- Sign in with your GitHub account
- Click "Authorize Railway"

### **Step 3: Click "New Project"**
- Select "Deploy from GitHub repo"
- Choose your `ReactProject` repository
- Click "Deploy"

**DONE! Your app is live. That's literally it.** ✅
